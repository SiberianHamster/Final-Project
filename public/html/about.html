We are Discover South Asia! Founded in 1985, Discover South Asia has been at the forefront of the adventure travel industry for three decades. We believe in providing quality expeditions and that give travelers an authentic experience while preserving the local culture and environment. We have a passion and deep respect for South Asia and it's people. We imerse our clients in the local culture but always act as considerate guests.

When you travel with Discover South Asia you are traveling with an award winning, industry leading, team of professionals here to give you the adventure of a lifetime. And with a combined 60 years of climbing, trekking, and guiding experience our expedition leaders are literally some of the best in the world.

Nepal

Beautiful and wild Nepal. From trekking to the Everest base camp to riding elephants in the Chitwan national park, this country has an assortment of activities that will both thrill and captivate the imagination!

Bali

Discover Indonesia’s best-known island, the ‘Land of Smiles’, in style, with the sophistication and local expertise of our sister company, Jacada Travel. We’ll help you enjoy the very best of Bali, according to your specifications, with an entirely bespoke luxury itinerary and an inspired choice of private tours, refined accommodation and personalised experiences.

Thailand

Discover our tour packages that encompass the stunning scenic and cultural delights of this unique country. Thailand has a natural asset in breath taking scenery, featuring spectacular green mountains, white sandy beaches, and sparking blue sea. Combine that with our world class range of forest and river expeditions, local dining and variety of cultural activites and you will experience Thailand at its greatest.
